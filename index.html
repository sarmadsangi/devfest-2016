The Web Development Bootcamp

The Web Development Bootcamp is a 12-week, in-person training built for people who have never written a single line of code in their lives.

In this 12-weeks, you’ll learn the principles and techniques for web design and development through daily lessons and practical sessions. You’ll also get real-world coding practice by working on 5 projects.

By the end of the 12 weeks, you’ll have a solid resume and the skills to get hired, freelance or even bootstrap your own startup.

Skills You'll Learn:

  • Design Fundamentals

    Learn typography, colors, grids and other design principles so you can produce respectable-looking websites and applications.

  • Frontend Web Development

    Familiarize yourself with HTML, CSS and JavaScript—languages that let's you decide how your visitors view and interact with your website.

  • Backend Web Development

    Discover what goes on beyond Frontend Web Development. You'll learn to build membership portals, get data from twitter and more.

  • Project Management

    Gain the know-how of designing and developing minimum viable products through both short-term and real-life projects.

  • Working with Teams

    Find out how developers collaborate and how they can work from anywhere in the world.
